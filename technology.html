A Platform Designed to Manage your Last Mile.

The “last mile” is the final step in a supply chain when a product is handed over to the customer, making it more sensitive to quality, performance and operational issues than any other step.

GrandJunction’s cloud-based software gives organizations real time visibility and control over their last mile logistics through a unique approach that proactively identifies issues at the local carrier before they impact the customer.

GrandJunction assists organizations in the last mile by providing proactive management; centralized visibility and coordination to enhance the customer experience and reduce delivery issues. Companies can now analyze and audit performance to reduce claims and drive continuous improvement, controlling 100% of the last mile spend.

So how can GrandJunction help? Check it out:

    Technology

  • Existing integrations to over 200 carrier dispatch systems build the foundation for comprehensive, real time visibility

  • Collaborative workflows streamline communication and improve accountability across internal teams and supply chain partners

    Operations

  • Real time view of order and delivery details including delivery stage, item detail, GPS location and proof of delivery

  • Exception-based alerts and dashboards allow carriers to proactively address potential delivery issues

    Customer Care

  • Configurable integration engine lets you share desired delivery information with end customers

  • Online case management allows tracking and resolution of delivery issues

    Performance

  • On demand performance metrics measure shortages, damage rates, on-time performance, claims, customer satisfaction and more

  • Driver level trending reports facilitate immediate and lasting improvement

    Spend

  • Manage all contract pricing terms across carriers in one location, including base rates, tiered pricing, fuel surcharges and accessorial rates

  • Reverse invoicing calculates order and POD information for error free invoice settlement

  • Drill into accessorial and surcharge fees to identify ways to reduce spend

GrandJunction Results

  • 8% savings in transportation spend
  • 10% reduction in customer service issues
  • 20% decrease in shortages & damages
  • 15% reduction in last mile driven operating expense
  • Exponential sales growth via enhanced customer experience
